Biography

Welcome to my personal portfolio! My name is Zhen Wang, also known as Ryan. I came to Australia four years ago to pursue my undergraduate degree, and I am now about to graduate with a Bachelor of Advanced Computing (Honours) from the Australian National University.

This website is a showcase of my life, education, and work experience. I have a passion for data science and machine learning, and I am eager to launch my career in this field. I hope to connect the dots of my journey over the past few years through a timeline that highlights my achievements and milestones.

In addition to my work, I have a love for basketball and try to play it every day. I used to go to the gym four times a week, but I have been neglecting this hobby lately due to my busy schedule. I am trying to pick it up again because I believe it's easier to continue doing something than to start from scratch. I also enjoy hiking, although my options are limited in Canberra. Thank you for visiting my website and learning more about me
